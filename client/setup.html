The aerosol testing apparatus comprises the following pieces of equipment:

1) BGI Collision Nebulizer (for KCl aerosol generation) with a speed controlled pump
2) TSI 9306 optical particle counter (OPC)
3) TerraBloom 4" duct fan with 15-100% speed controller. Airflow: 160 CFM; Power 18W; Pressure: 334 Pa.
4) EXTECH 45158 mini thermo-anemometer: airflow/temperature/relative humidity meter
5) DWYER 475 differential gauge, 0-4.982 kPa
The air duct is designed to test a small-scale purifier developed by our lab and now modified to test small samples of material (50 mm diameter disks), or small air purifier that can build an air-tight connection to the air duct.
